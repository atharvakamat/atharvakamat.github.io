About

I am a PhD candidate in theoretical chemistry interested in designing and scaling molecular and material simulations with quantum accuracy. Currently, I am creating my own quantum dynamics simulator and collaborating on property prediction models. My research has the end goal of enabling accurate large scale material/chemical predictions and investigating related quantum field theories.

Prior to Princeton, I performed experimental research as an undergrad in condensed matter physics, biomedical engineering, and materials science, while studying mathematical approaches to quantum gravity out of interest.

Outside of work, I enjoy working out, reading, and eating :)

Please reach out to me if you would like to collaborate or chat about research.
